My Dear Brother
I received your Gind Letter and orders the 16 & the next
day I went to the Rost office they told me the order was
for Plymouth Dock I told my Master & must goe to Doele
and he was very willing I should lye he told ellestress that I
Must Goe to Dock but She said I should not goe till such
time as I had met away the Clothes for it was our
washing wick we wish once in three weeks so I finished

Doost office I showed them the order they ask: "my name was
Elizth Clift I said it was they told me to sine the order so
